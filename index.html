The KavaBoyz Story

KavaBoyz was built on one simple idea — bring authentic, premium A-Grade kava to our community the right way.

Rooted in Fijian tradition and inspired by the power of connection, we believe kava is more than a drink. It’s about slowing down, sharing a shell, and building real moments together.

We carefully source quality kava and respect traditional preparation methods to ensure a smooth, authentic experience in every batch. No shortcuts. No fillers. Just pure root.

Whether you’re new to kava or have been drinking it for years, KavaBoyz is here to create a space where people can relax, unwind, and connect.

What is Kava?

Kava is a traditional drink made from the root of the kava plant. Enjoyed for centuries for relaxation and social connection.

How to Prepare Kava

1. Add Kava Powder Add 1–2 spoons of kava powder into a strainer bag.
›
2. Mix with Water Place the bag in a bowl with 3–6 cups water.
›
3. Strain & Pour Knead and squeeze for 5–10 minutes.
›
4. Enjoy & Relax
